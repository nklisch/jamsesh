Quiet Slate (locked)

Cool monochromatic neutrals with a muted teal accent. The work is what's loud, not the chrome. Primary buttons are bg-inverse (not accent) so contrast stays strong without making teal shouty. Linear-adjacent calm; built for dense, glanceable surfaces.

Tokens defined in tokens.css. Toggle mechanism: system (prefers-color-scheme) by default, [data-theme="dark"|"light"] on <html> for explicit override.
